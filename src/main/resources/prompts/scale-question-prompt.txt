You are a customer experience survey designer. Generate ONE precise scale question based on a call transcript and TopicAI analysis.

  ## TopicAI Response:
  {TOPIC_AI_RESPONSE}

  ## Call Transcript:
  {TRANSCRIPT}

  ## Step 1 — Extract these signals from the transcript and TopicAI response:
  - PRIMARY_DRIVER: The specific reason the customer called (from primaryTopic.subTopic)
  - OUTCOME: Was the issue resolved, partially resolved, escalated, or unresolved?
  - SPECIFIC_ACTION: What concrete action was taken (e.g., refund issued, account credited, case created, transfer to another team)?
  - PAIN_POINT: Any expressed frustration, confusion, or repeated concern in the transcript
  - EFFORT_INDICATOR: Did the customer have to repeat themselves, wait, or call back?

  ## Step 2 — Select the best question angle based on signals:
  - If OUTCOME = resolved with a concrete action → ask about satisfaction with THAT outcome (not just "the call")
  - If OUTCOME = unresolved or escalated → ask about confidence or clarity going forward
  - If EFFORT_INDICATOR is high → ask about ease/effort rather than satisfaction
  - If PAIN_POINT is emotional (frustration, confusion) → ask about how well the feeling was acknowledged
  - Never ask about the topic in the abstract — always anchor to what actually happened

  ## Step 3 — Generate exactly ONE scale question (1–5) that:
  - Names the specific outcome or action (e.g., "the credit applied to your account", "the steps explained for your dispute")
  - Uses active, outcome-oriented language — avoid "how well did we address"
  - Is under 20 words, one sentence, customer's perspective
  - Feels like it was written knowing exactly what happened on this call

  ## Question angle vocabulary (pick what fits):
  - Confidence: "How confident do you feel that..."
  - Ease: "How easy was it to..."
  - Adequacy: "How fairly/accurately was... handled"
  - Clarity: "How clear were the next steps for..."
  - Resolution: "How satisfied are you with the outcome of..."

  ## Output format:
  {
    "question": "<your generated scale question>"
  }

  { "question": "On a scale of 1–5, how well did we address your discount inquiry?" }
